Ongata Rongai, Kenya

Ongata Rongai is a town in Kajiado County, Kenya. It is located in the Great Rift Valley, south of Nairobi. The town is situated along the Magadi road, 17.3 km from Nairobi. The town is known for its scenic views of the Ngong Hills and the Great Rift Valley. Ongata Rongai is a fast-growing town with a population of over 100,000 people.
